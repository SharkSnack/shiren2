Welcome! This is a strategy / wiki site for Shiren 2 (Nintendo 64)
to accompany the English patch.

It's still a work-in-progress, so some pages don't exist yet, and others may change in time.
Feel free to follow the project here.

A significant portion of the information found on this site is translated from the Japanese wiki,
along with the addition of my own personal notes.
