Castle

As the story progresses, Shiren soon finds himself having to build a castle for the village.
Castle Materials can be found throughout Shuten Mountain, and each category comes in three grades.

The basic flow comes down to:
Obtain materials in dungeons → Make castle parts at the summit → Return to the village and build.

Note that your save file will receive a castle icon when you complete the castle.
