Castle

As the story progresses, Shiren soon finds himself having to build a castle for the village.
Castle Materials can be found throughout Shuten Mountain, and each category comes in three grades.

The basic flow is:
Obtain materials in dungeons → Make castle parts at the summit → Return to the village and build.

Note that your save file will receive a castle icon when you complete the castle.

How to Build

There's a Processing Shop on the second floor of Shuten Summit, before the Raft Shop.
Talk to the person behind the counter there to turn castle materials into castle parts.
Then simply ride the raft back to Napus Village, and Shiren will automatically build the part.
Building takes an entire day, and you can't enter dungeons until you finish building the parts you brought.

As the story progresses, Oni will occasionally attack the castle, potentially breaking sections of the castle.
The way to counter that is to use higher grade materials to build break-resistant castle parts.

Castle Materials

The 5 categories of materials are "Soil", "Water", "Wood", "Rock", and "Iron Sand",
and the 3 grades of materials are "Normal", "Good", and "Perfect".
These materials can be used as regular items as well. (Materials page for details).

On a side note, Perfect grade materials take up 2 inventory slots.
Materials cannot be inserted into pots, but can be deposited by Mamo.
The locations of each category and grade of material is shown in the following table:

As you can see, Perfect grade castle materials can be found in Shuten Trail Hard,
while Shuten Trail Easy only has Normal grade materials.
However, note that Perfect Wood can only be found in Shuten Trail Mid.
It's easy to forget, so try to remember that when going on adventures.
Also note that Perfect grade materials can only be found on floors after Shuten Halfway.

Lastly, enemy monsters will never drop castle materials when tripped,
such as when using an ally Armordillo's special attack.

Castle Parts

The 5 categories of castle materials can be combined in different combinations to make castle parts.
The combinations for each type of castle part are:

Part Materials
Honmaru Soil, Water, Wood, Rock, Iron Sand
Ninomaru Soil, Wood, Iron Sand
Inner Wall Soil, Wood
Outer Wall Soil, Water
Moat Water, Rock

Completing a castle section requires 4 of the same castle part.
So at minimum, you need 16 Soil, 12 Water, 12 Wood, 8 Rock, and 8 Iron Sand to complete the castle.
Prioritize Soil, Water, and Wood if you find yourself having to choose what to carry with a full inventory.

The grade of materials determines the grade of castle parts.
The 4 castle part grades are "Normal", "Resistant", "Sturdy", and "Unbreaking".
Using all Perfect grade materials leads to an Unbreaking grade castle part,
which just as the name implies, will never break as a result of Oni attacks.

Also, if the entire castle is built using Perfect grade parts, the castle will become a Golden Castle.
It's possible to upgrade parts even after the castle is completed, so you don't need to build it using
Perfect materials from the very beginning, but it's a fun challenge to go for at some point.

Oni Attacks

As you build the castle and progress the story, Oni will begin to launch attacks on the castle.
The attack event itself is automatic, so there's no gameplay or fighting involved.
As a result of the attack, a castle section built using lower grade parts might be destroyed.
It's best to use higher grade castle parts so that you don't end up having to rebuild sections.

On a side note, a Perfect grade castle material can be found after the first Oni attack at the castle site.

Post-Completion

Completing the castle grants access to Onigashima, and the ending plays after clearing that dungeon.
Once you enter post-game, a couple of new ways to interact with your castle become available.

Castle Decorations

A variety of castle decorations can be obtained from post-game story events and dungeons.
Collecting these castle decorations and displaying them is part of the fun of post-game in Shiren 2.
See the Castle Decorations page for details on where to obtain each decoration.

Changing Parts・Golden Castle

You'll be able to upgrade sections of your castle using higher grade castle parts.
See what happens when you build a Golden Castle by using Perfect grade castle parts for all sections.

1. Level Up at the Training Hall

2. Ride the Cart to Shuten Halfway
Deposit all of your items to Mamo, and don't forget to bring Mamo along.
3. Proceed to Shuten Mid or Hard for Perfect Materials
Use Scout Bracelet to locate items, and take shortcuts using Phantasm Bracelet.
Kengo is the only monster you need to worry about.
Each run can take 8 minutes or less if you move optimally and take the shortest paths to items.

It's possible to return to 11F in Shuten Trail Mid by using a Pitfall, so one way to quickly collect
Perfect Wood would be to bring a Trapper Bracelet and numerous Pitfalls to repeat 11~12F.
