Monster House Characteristics

Monster House is a location in a dungeon where a room is packed with many monsters and items.
They are generated at random, and can be generated in dungeons starting with Onigashima.
The ground is also covered in hidden traps, so care is needed when moving around the room.

It's best to eat Sight Grass if you have it on hand, but otherwise check for traps one tile at a time.
If you successfully clear out a Monster House, you can claim the items as a reward.
Whether Monster Houses are positive or negative generally comes down to a player's skill.

The message "It's a (Monster Type) House!" is displayed when you first step foot into a Monster House,
and the music changes to one of two Monster House themes, depending on the type.

Every monster in the room will wake up the moment you enter the room,
and the location of all monsters on the current floor becomes visible on the map. (Like Scout Bracelet)

Special Monster Houses can include high level monsters outside of the dungeon's normal monster table,
making them extremely dangerous. Though that also works in reverse, where Special Monster Houses
in deeper floors can contain low level monsters, possibly making them easier than regular Monster Houses.

Note that Statues will never be generated inside of a Monster House.
The developers likely realized how chaotic it would be if a Bomber Statue or Trip Statue were present.

Monster House Strategy

Detecting Beforehand

Multiple monsters or items visible near room entrance
There's a chance the room is a Monster House.
The likeliness increases further if you shoot an arrow into the room and hit an unseen monster.
Avoid entering the room if you don't have any items needed to take on a Monster House.
Room with many monsters visible using a Scout Bracelet
Guaranteed to be a Monster House.
Skip entering the room if you don't have any items needed to tackle a Monster House.
Check the sleeping monsters with the bracelet equipped to determine the type of Monster House.
The "It's a Monster House!" message randomly appeared
On rare occasions, a Dosukoi family monster can use its special attack to shift one of the sleeping monsters
outside of the Monster House, which results in the Monster House activating even if you're nowhere near it.
It's particularly annoying when there's a Landmine in the room, since a shifted item or monster could end up
landing on top of the Landmine, triggering an explosion and wiping out some of the items.

Good Items for Monster Houses

If you detect a Monster House beforehand
Thin out the number of monsters in the room before entering, using items such as:
Bracelets: Pierce, Bend, Phantasm
Projectiles: Wooden Arrow, Iron Arrow, Razor Arrow, Porky Stone, Cannonball

Picking off monsters one at a time from outside of the room is an excellent way to give yourself an advantage.
Monsters that receive any damage will instantly wake up, so be mindful of that when using items that pierce.
If you unknowingly step into a Monster House
Take one step back so you end up in a hallway, only having to fight one enemy at a time.
You can hit three monsters per attack at the entrance if you have a Razor Wind or Morning Star.
If there are multiple entrances, or monsters with ranged attacks are present, take another step back.
You'll be safe from ranged attacks, and monsters that lost sight of Shiren will wander somewhere else.
Clearing out the Monster House
Use the items mentioned below to give yourself an advantage!

Weapons

三 Seal (Razor Wind)
Swing your weapon at a room's entrance to deal damage to three enemies at once.
Simple yet powerful seal that should be synthesized onto any main weapon whenever possible.
Morning Star
Two-handed weapon that can be used like Razor Wind.
You might be tempted to remain in the Monster House to deal 5 hits, but it's far too dangerous with 0 defense.
Spear
Regular attacks can pierce through enemies 2 tiles forward.
As long as you don't miss, you can defeat enemies without damage by taking 2 steps back at the beginning.
回 Seal (Healing Sword)
Excellent for restoring HP during extended fights.
The standard must-have seal for any main weapon, along with the 三 seal (Razor Wind).
Type Effective Weapons (Drain Buster, Ghost Sickle, etc.)
These seals shine when you're up against Special Monster Houses.
Pickaxe, Wonder Pickaxe
Dig around the edge of the room before entering a Monster House to thin out monsters along the walls.
Be careful not to dig too much, resulting in an expanded room with nowhere to run.

Shields

見 Seal (Evasive Shield)
Increased evasion means you take less damage during extended fights.
バ Seal (Counter Shield)
You can get by without it, but it helps increase damage output during extended fights.
弟 Seal (Otogiriso)
Effectively reduces the amount of damage you take.
眠 Seal (Slumber Scroll)
Helps if you don't have enough attack power to defeat enemies in a single hit.
Can also help to shut down monsters that move or attack through walls.

Bracelets

Pierce Bracelet
Can be used to thin out the number of monsters before entering the room,
or to clear out the line of monsters that chase after you as a result of waking the room up.
Watch out for Shihan when relying on bracelets such as this one, since it has the ability to disarm bracelets.
Check out the How to Use Pierce Bracelet section for more tips.
Bend Bracelet
Can be used to attack monsters along the walls before entering the room.
Strength Bracelet
Higher strength means you're more likely to defeat enemies in a single hit.
Phantasm Bracelet
Can be used to defeat monsters along the walls before entering the room.
Hide inside a wall after waking the room up to clear the room without taking damage.
Trapper Bracelet
Monster Houses are full of traps, so enemies will trigger traps left and right.
However, note that dangerous traps like Landmine or Flood Trap could be present,
and Shiren's attack is drastically lowered while he has Trapper Bracelet equipped.

Scrolls

Trap Activate Scroll
Similar to Trapper Bracelet, but guaranteed to activate all traps in the room when read.
Always keep in mind that you could end up triggering a Landmine or Flood Trap.
Lightning Scroll
Deals roughly 30 damage to all enemies in the room.
One scroll is enough to clear out an entire Monster House early on, but it isn't as effective on deeper floors.
That said, it can still be a nice way to soften enemies up so that you can defeat them in a single attack.
Blaze Scroll
Deals 20 damage per turn to any character standing in the fire that generates from reading the scroll.
Items on the ground will burn up, and tiles with traps will not be set on fire.
It's best to read this in a hallway to minimize item loss.
Slumber Scroll
Puts all monsters in the room to sleep for 10 turns.
Note that mosnters will move at double speed after waking up from this effect.
It's best to read this when the exit is in the middle of a Monster House, rather than rely on it to clear one out.
Decoy Scroll
Provides a big advantage while inside the room, but only lasts for 10 turns.
It's best to either defeat monsters quickly, or retreat before it wears off and put yourself in a better position.
Avoid reading it when you don't have enough space to summon shadows.
Trap Remove Scroll
Nice to read after clearing out the Monster House.
Fear Scroll
Plays well with the 三 seal (Razor Wind).
Can be used to have a monster defeat another monster, but you'd need a way to defeat the leveled up monster.
