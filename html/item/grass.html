Revival Grass turns into this grass after taking effect.

Herb (薬草)

Restores 25 HP. Increases max HP by 2 points if HP is already at max.
Can be thrown at Ghost type monsters to deal 25 damage.
The 薬 seal restores a small amount of HP when you attack (weapon) or take damage (shield), and is one of the three seals used in synthesizing a Healing Sword.

Swift Grass (すばやさ草)

Increases movement speed by 1 stage for 12 turns.
Max movement speed stage is +2, where you get 3 actions per turn.
If thrown at a monster, the target will gain a permanent movement speed boost.

Warp Grass (高とび草)

Warps you to a random room on the current floor when eaten.
Can be thrown at a monster to warp them instead.

Shrink Seed (胃縮小の種)

Lowers max fullness by 10% when eaten. The lowest max fullness value is 0%.
Nothing good will come from keeping it on hand, so sell it, insert it into a Presto Pot, or throw it away.

Antidote Grass (毒消し草)

Restores lost strength to max when eaten.
Can be thrown at Slug monsters to deal 50 damage.
The 消 seal is equivalent to the う seal from a Scale Shield, and blocks poison.
Note that the seal cannot block Slug licks.

Disaster Seed (超不幸の種)

Lowers level to 1 and reduces current HP to 1 when eaten.
Provides the 超 seal if synthesized, which curses the equipment and lowers your level by 1 every time you attack (weapon) or take damage (shield).
Possibly the most dangerous item in the game, but only appears in shops inside the "Roat to the End" dungeon 71F+.

Locations:

  • Road to the End (Shop)
Buy 400 Sell 200
Seal 超 Stack No

Talk Seed (話の種)

Makes you tell jokes, superstitions, serial novels, and one-liners, which deals a random negative effect to all characters in the room.
Effects include slow, sleep, confusion, paralysis, freeze, anger NPCs, and ~30 damage.
If no one else is in the room, you become the target of the negative effect.

Locations:

  • Shrine's Hidden Hole (Floor)
  • Sealed Room (Floor, Presto)
  • Road to the End (Floor, Presto, Surprise, Shop, Trader)

Boost Grass (よくきき草)

Doubles effectiveness of grass items for 10 turns when eaten.
Often used before strength grass.
Doubles the effectiveness of Grass Gainer, Grass Tosser, and Grass Effect Pot.
Note that some grasses don't gain boosted effects, such as Revival Grass, Sight Grass, Antidote Grass, etc.

Locations:

Revival Grass (復活の草)

Automatically revives you if your HP drops to 0 inside of a dungeon.
Doesn't activate if it's inside of a pot.
Turns into Weeds after taking effect.
HP, strength, and fullness is restored to max upon reviving.
Mr. Gyro has a 75% chance to drop this grass.
