Bracelet Details

Level Lock Bracelet (くねくねよけの腕輪)

Protects against the level down effects of Twisty Hani's dance and Gazelle Pope spells.
Doesn't prevent level down effects from eating or getting hit by Grasses.

Locations:

  • Onigashima (Floor, Presto)
  • Road to the End (Floor)

Pierce Bracelet (遠投の腕輪)

Thrown items will fly forever, piercing through walls and monsters.
Be careful not to accidentally throw important items while it's equipped.
Useful for clearing out a Monster House from outside the room or lines of monsters in hallways, but can cause accidents if the thrown item or projectile hits a Shopkeeper.

Locations:

  • Shuten Trail Easy (Presto)
  • Shuten Trail Mid (Presto, Trader)
  • Shuten Trail Hard (Floor, Presto, Trader)
  • Onigashima (Floor, Presto, Surprise, Shop, Trader)

Dragon Tile Bracelet (白発白中の腕輪)

Provides a 中 seal which is needed for synthesizing Daisangen equipment.

Scout Bracelet (とうしの腕輪)

Parry Bracelet (うけながしの腕輪)

Redirects damage from physical attacks to a nearby monster.
If there are no other monsters nearby, you'll take damage as normal.
The attacking monster will level up if it defeats another monster due to this effect.
Bracelets will still take damage when successfully redirecting attacks.
Asuka will not redirect attacks when she has this bracelet equipped.

Locations:

  • Sealed Room (Golden Interval)
  • Road to the End (Shop)
Buy 15000 Sell 7500
Seal - Stack -
Phrase -

No Hunger Bracelet (ハラヘラズの腕輪)

Stops hunger. No negative side effects.
Doesn't prevent HP loss if your fullness is already at 0%.
Note that it only stops natural hunger depletion, so Hunger Puppet songs will still lower fullness.

Locations:

Trapper Bracelet (ワナ師の腕輪)

Lets you pick up and set traps, and monsters that step on traps will trigger them.
Cracks more easily than other bracelets. (See durability values for details)
Note that defeating a Trader using traps triggers thief mode.
Pair it with a Scout Bracelet to display all traps on the current floor on the map.
It's not mentioned in the description, but modifiers that lower attack and exp gain are applied like inside the "Halfway Well" dungeon while it's equipped.

Locations:

  • Halfway Well (Clear Reward)
