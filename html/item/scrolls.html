Identifies an unidentified item, revealing its name and uses if it's a staff.

Lightning Scroll (いかずちの巻物)

Lightning strikes all monsters in the room, dealing 25~30 each.
However, Oni are immune to lightning and are healed by it.
Lightning will strike all monsters in a 1 tile radius if you're in a hallway.
Lightning cannot hit monsters inside walls.

Blaze Scroll (炎上の巻物)

Creates fire pillars around Shiren that deal 20 damage per turn.
Great for wiping out monster houses early on, but note that you might end up burning allies and items as well.
Some enemies are also immune to fire damage, such as Tiny Phoenix.

Retreat Scroll (引き上げの巻物)

Escape from a dungeon with your Gitan and currently equipped items.
Cannot escape if you're being chased by guard dogs and sheriffs.
The 帰 seal lets you keep the weapon or shield it's synthesized onto if you collapse.
It can't prevent equipment loss by other means, such as Kengo monsters flinging it away.
Always found identified.

Locations:

Purify Scroll (おはらいの巻物)

Removes curses from currently equipped items.
Also removes curses from Asuka's equipment if she is standing next to you.
The 祓 seal prevents your weapon from getting cursed (weapon) or prevents all items from getting cursed (shield).
There's a bug where if you synthesize it onto equipment with the 呪 seal, the 呪 seal gets deleted and it also doesn't gain the 祓 seal.

Decoy Scroll (分身の巻物)

Summons up to 4 shadows that distract enemies for 10 turns.
The shadows won't attack enemies, but they can draw the attention of almost any attack, including Archdragon's floor-wide fire breath.
However, you still might get hit by ranged attacks that pass through the shadows, such as Ironhead's attacks or Pop Tank's cannonballs.

Slumber Scroll (バクスイの巻物)

Puts all monsters in the room to sleep for 10 turns.
Monsters will move twice as fast when they wake up.
Cannot put monsters inside walls to sleep.
The 眠 seal can put a monster to sleep for 5 turns when you attack it (weapon) or receive damage from it (shield).

Evaporate Scroll (水がれの巻物)

Evaporates all water on the current floor. Ice will not evaporate.
Water-only monsters will be unable to move, and some won't even be able to attack.
Good to have for recovering items that fall into water.
Useful for capturing underwater monsters such as Shotfish.

Explosion Scroll (バクハの巻物)

Deals damage in a similar fashion to Landmines.
Place it on the ground and perform a regular attack to make it explode.
Note that this is considered as an attack by Shiren, so if you blow up shop items or NPCs such as Traders, you'll be chased by Guard Dogs and Sherrifs.

Locations:

Wall Collapse Scroll (大部屋の巻物)

Transforms the current floor into one giant room when read.
Water, ice, and unbreakable walls all vanish.
Best way to search for Golden Intervals, and also makes it easier to steal from shops.
However, some care must be taken when reading it, or else you might find yourself waking up a monster house, or being under the effect of floor-wide statue effects.

Locations:

  • Onigashima (Floor, Presto, Surprise, Trader)
  • Shrine's Hidden Hole (Floor)
  • Halfway Well (Floor)
  • Sealed Room (Floor, Presto)
  • Road to the End (Floor, Presto, Surprise, Trader)

Sturdy Pot Scroll (壺われずの巻物)

Makes the selected pot unbreakable for the duration of the current adventure.
Good for protecting pots such as storage, water, and monster pots, but can be detrimental if used on pots such as sticky or synthesis pots.
In particular, you'll instantly get a game over if a Kappa monster throws an unbreakable sticky pot at you, even if you have revival grass on hand.

Locations:

  • Shrine's Hidden Hole (Floor)

Plating Scroll (メッキの巻物)

Makes a weapon or shield rustproof with a red 金 seal. Cannot remove curses.
Can also repair bracelets, or transform iron arrows into silver arrows.
Monsoon Dolls will tear off a red 金 seal before any other seal, so this scroll effectively protects your synthesized seals against a single use of Monsoon Doll's special attack.

Restock Scroll (仕入れ直しの巻物)

Restocks a shop to a max of 9 items for sale.
Specialty shops will only restock items within the same category.
You may need to expand the shop's size by breaking its walls to get all 9 items in the restock if the room is small.
Note that N'dubbas can be included in the restock.

Locations:

  • Onigashima (Trader)
  • Sealed Room (Presto)
  • Road to the End (Floor, Presto, Surprise, Trader)

Blank Scroll (白紙の巻物)

Select the name of any scroll you've read before, and this scroll will act as that scroll.
You can revert the scroll back to a blank state by splashing water on it.
When used as synthesis fodder with a Mixer monster, the equipment's slot count will increase by 1, up to a max of 16 slots.

Locations:

  • Onigashima (Shop, Trader)

Sanctuary Scroll (聖域の巻物)

Place this scroll on the ground to create a safe zone where monsters next to the scroll cannot attack anything standing on top of the scroll.
Monsters also cannot walk on top of the scroll.
Note that you can still get hit by ranged attacks while standing on the scroll.
No effect if read instead of being placed on the ground.

Locations:

  • Shuten Trail Easy (Presto)
  • Shuten Trail Mid (Presto, Shop, Trader)
  • Shuten Trail Hard (Floor, Presto, Surprise, Shop, Trader, Golden Interval)

Sanctaruy Scroll (聖城の巻物)

Resembles a Sanctuary Scroll.
Makes stairs and all items on the current floor hop around when placed on the ground.
Shop items won't jump around, but disguised N'dubbas will.
You can get rid of the effect by splashing water on the scroll (Water Pot) or burning the scroll (Fire Staff, Blaze Scroll).

Locations:

  • Sealed Room (Floor)
  • Road to the End (Floor, Shop)

Extinction Scroll (ねだやしの巻物)

Throw at a monster to make all instances of it vanish from the current adventure.
Note that other ranks in the family line will still appear.
Example: banish Pit Mamel → Mamel and Cave Mamel still appear.
Also note that only 1 monster can be banished at a time, so if a second scroll is thrown at a monster, the first monster will start appearing again.

Locations:

  • Road to the End (Shop)
