Wooden Shield (きたえた木の盾)

Low defense, but comes with an upgrade value ranging from +4 to +8.
It is never found cursed. Use as synthesis fodder to strengthen a stronger shield.

Locations:

  • Shuten Trail Hard (Surprise)
  • Onigashima (Trader, Treasure Chest)
  • Sealed Room (Presto, Treasure Chest)
  • Road to the End (Presto, Treasure Chest)

Tribal Shield (ばん族の盾)

Shield said to be passed down from a foreign tribe.
Can be useful as a synthesis seal container since it doesn't have an innate ability.
It's a bit too weak to use as a main shield.

Fuuma Shield (風魔の盾)

Shield with high defense. Good option as a main shield.
Insert a Fuuma Shield+99 into a Presto Pot to transform it into a Helix Shield, though note that upgrade value and seals will reset if you do so.

Shield made out of gold. Low defense, but it will never rust.

Shield that allows you to dodge projectiles. Makes it very easy to gather arrows.

The Reflect seal takes priority if you also have that synthesized.

Shield modeled after a Zalokleft.
Prevents item and Gitan theft by Zalokleft, Gamarra, and Gyadon monsters.

Disposable Shield (使い捨ての盾)

Starts out with very high defense, but loses 1 upgrade value every time you take damage.
Fixed damage such as quarter staff will still lower its upgrade value, but traps, poison grass, surprise pot explosions, and fire pillars won't.

Locations:
