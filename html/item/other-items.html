Other Items Details

Voucher (割引券)

Provides a 2% discount when buying items from shops. Up to 40 vouchers can be used per purchase. (80% off). Can be used a projectile, but only deals 1 damage. Traders will drop up to 25 vouchers when defeated.

Locations:

  • Shuten Trail Mid (M)
  • Shuten Trail Hard (M)
  • Onigashima (M)
  • Sealed Room (M)
  • Road to the End (M)

Water (水)

Water inside of a Water Pot. It has many uses, including the ability to rust enemy weapons, spoil riceballs, destroy traps, and put out fires. Try splashing it on all sorts of things!

Locations:

  • Inventory after scooping water into a Water Pot.

N'dubba (ンドゥバ)

N'dubba monster disguised as an item. Move an ally on top of a N'dubba, and then swap places with them to pick it up. Can be inserted into pots like other items, and Nigiri Morphs can transform it into a riceball. Throw it or place it on the ground to have it transform back into its monster form.

Locations:

  • Disguised as items in various dungeons.

Gitan (ギタン)

Money. Can be thrown to deal damage equal to the amount of gitan divided by 10. Deals a minimum of 1 damage, and a maximum of 9,999 damage.
