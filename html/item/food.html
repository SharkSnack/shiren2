Food Details

Spoiled Riceball (まずそうなおにぎり)

Restores 35% fullness, but deals 4 damage and comes with a negative side effect.
Negative effects include level-1, strength-3, confusion, sleep, and blindness.
Increases max fullness by 1% if full.

Locations:

  • Shuten Trail Mid (Floor)
  • Shuten Trail Hard (Floor)
  • Onigashima (Floor)
  • Sealed Room (Floor)
  • Road to the End (Floor, Shop)

Riceball (おにぎり)

Restores 50% fullness. Increases max fullness by 3% if full.
Provides the 飯 seal when synthesized onto equipment.
Commonly synthesized onto a secondary weapon early game to help avoid starvation.

Large Riceball (大きなおにぎり)

Restores 100% fullness. Increases max fullness by 5% if full.
You'll always start with a Large Riceball when entering dungeons.
Nigiri Morph monsters transform items into this riceball.

Locations:

  • Shuten Trail Easy (Floor)

Specialty Riceball (特製おにぎり)

Restores 30% fullness, and comes with a positive side effect.
Positive effects include strength+1, HP recovery, item identification, sleep immunity, walk without waking sleeping monsters, stop hunger, and poison immunity.
Increases max fullness by 2% if full.

Locations:

  • Onigashima (Presto, Shop)
  • Sealed Room (Floor)
  • Road to the End (Floor, Shop)

Giant Riceball (巨大なおにぎり)

Restores fullness to max, and also increases max fullness by 7%.
The best riceball in the game. Keep it safe from spoiling by placing it inside a holding pot.
