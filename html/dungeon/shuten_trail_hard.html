Shuten Trail Hard

Dungeon you can enter after clearing Shuten Trail Mid.

Show Saigo the Torn Manuscript over at the beach West of
Napus Village to transform it into the Ancient Manuscript,
and then place the Manuscript on the small shrine next to
the waterfall in the upper-right corner of Napus Village.

There are a total of 15 floors with 2 of them being
boss fights, and there is a carry-in limit of 15 items.
You can bring any number of allies with you.

Just as the name implies, many stronger monsters that
weren't present in the first two trails will make an appearance.

However, the items you find in this dungeon are generally
better than the first two trails, and you can synthesize using
Mixer monsters, so it's worth entering despite the added challenge.

It's possible to clear this dungeon without carry-in items,
but you'll likely struggle if you're not a seasoned wanderer.
