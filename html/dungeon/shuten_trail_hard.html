Shuten Trail Hard

Dungeon you can enter after clearing Shuten Trail Mid.

Show Saigo the Torn Manuscript over at the beach West of Napus Village
to transform it into the Ancient Manuscript, and then place the Manuscript
on the small shrine next to the waterfall in the upper-right corner of Napus Village.

The dungeon has a total of 15 floors with 2 boss fights, and a carry-in limit of 11 items.
You can bring any number of allies with you.

Just as the name implies, many stronger monsters that
weren't present in the first two trails will make an appearance.

However, the items you find in this dungeon are generally better than
the first two trails, and you can synthesize using Mixer monsters,
so it's worth entering despite the added challenge.

It's possible to clear this dungeon without carry-in items,
but you'll likely struggle if you're not a seasoned wanderer.

※ Perfect Wood cannot be found on this trail.

Dungeon Overview

Four key points:

  1. Bracelets can be found on the ground in an unidentified state on this trail.
    Bracelets offer powerful effects when equipped that can help you on your adventures
    such as Scout Bracelet, which allows you to see where items and enemies are on the map.
    Note that bracelets can shatter in Shiren 2, so unequip them when you don't need their effects.

  2. Synthesis is now possible, thanks to Mixer monsters and Synthesis Pots being available.
    Mixer appears starting on 9F, so you can synthesize there using items you picked up in earlier trails.
    Note that unlike Mixer monsters, Synthesis Pots cannot combine different categories. (ex: Weapon + Grass)
    If you have a Pierce Bracelet equipped, be sure to unequip it before throwing a Synthesis Pot at a wall.

  3. Golden Intervals can be found starting with this dungeon.
    Golden Stairs may be buried in a wall between floors 3~6F, which lead to the Golden Interval area.
    You likely don't have a way to break walls to search for the stairs at this point in the game, but once you
    reach Onigashima, you can obtain Wall Collapse Scrolls, which turns the current floor into one giant room.

  4. The first half of Shuten Trail Hard has "Good" grade materials,
    and "Perfect" grade materials can be found during the second half.
    "Perfect" grade materials take up 2 inventory slots, so it can be helpful to bring Mamo along.
    If you simply want to hurry through the story, the most efficient route would be
    Shuten Trail Hard for the first half, and Shuten Trail Mid for the second half.

Important Items

Weapon

Aim to create a Katana+5 with the 三 and 回 seals as a base.
You can then select from options like ド, 仏, 目, or 幸 for the remaining 3 synthesis slots.
If 回 is too difficult, you can substitute it with multiple 弟.

Seal Item Info
三 Razor Wind Lets you attack in a 3-way pattern.
回 Healing Sword Heal HP equal to 1/3 of the amount of damage you dealt.
Synthesize Herb, Otogiriso, and Life Grass onto a weapon to turn it into a Healing Sword.
ド Drain Buster Deal x1.5 damage to Drain monsters. (ex: Slugs, Puppets, Scorpion)
仏 Ghost Sickle Deal x1.5 damage to Ghost monsters. (ex: Yamikichi, Gale, Pumphantasm)
目 Cyclops Killer Deal x1.5 damage to One-eyed monsters. (ex: Ironhead, Golem, Gazer)
弟 Otogiriso Restore a fixed amount of HP when you deal damage. (4, 7, 10, 13, 15)
幸 Happy Grass Gain more exp when you defeat monsters. (+10%, +40%, +70%, +100%, +130%)
You'll want at least 2~3 seals to make it noteworthy.

You might be able to obtain a Dotanuki or Spark Sword from a Surprise Pot on 15F of this trail.
If you do so, go ahead and switch to using it as a main weapon instead of a Katana.

You could also use Secret Phrases to obtain items like Manji-Kabura, Homing Blade, and Angel Seed
to create a powerful weapon very early, but it does break the balance of the game if you do so.

Shield

Aim to create a Beetle Shield+5 with one 皮 and at least 3 弟 seals as a base.
Other options are somewhat limited at this point in the game,
but you could add a バ seal to increase your damage output a bit.

Seal Item Info
皮 Leather Shield Halves hunger rate.
弟 Otogiriso Restore a fixed amount of HP when you receive damage. (4, 7, 10, 13, 15)
バ Counter Shield Reflect 30% of the damage you received back at the enemy.

You could use Leather Shield as a base instead of a Beetle Shield too,
if you want to prevent the loss of upgrade value due to rust.

You could also use Secret Phrases to obtain a powerful Fuuma Shield early,
but it does break the balance of the game if you do so.

Floor Guide

01~02F Waterfall Basin (Shuffle)

Monsters include Mamel, Pit Mamel, and Chintala

You shouldn't have much trouble dealing with them at this point.

If you're feeling adventurous, you could throw Happy Grass at a Pit Mamel
to level it up into a Cave Mamel, and then either use a Knockback Staff or
throw Gitan or a shield with at least 5 defense to one-shot it and gain 500 experience.

Recommended to get Shiren up to level 4 before proceeding to 3F.

03~04F Mountain Cave

Piko Hammer and Slug make an appearance starting on 3F.

Piko Hammer's attacks deal ~10 damage if you don't have a shield equipped.

Slugs appear between 3~5F, and can lower Shiren's strength with their licks,
so use projectiles to defeat them from a distance if possible.

There's a 30% chance for Golden Stairs to be hidden inside a wall between 3~6F.
See the Golden Interval section of this page for more details.

05~06F Hoof Rock Wall

Hunt Zaloklefts between 6~8F for items.

Watch out for Bow Boy's ranged attacks.

07~08F Dark Night Cavern

This is where monsters begin to hit pretty hard.

Tiger Tosser, Boy Cart, and Warukichi can deal 15~20 damage per hit if your shield is weak.

If the generated map is one large room, head for the stairs instead of trying to collect all items.

Continue to hunt Zaloklefts for items if you find yourself able to take on the other monsters.

Shuten Halfway

Hone your weapon, and buy HP recovery items and food from the Trader's Shop.

The Blacksmith can erase synthesis seals or mend cracked bracelets, so keep that in mind
if you accidentally synthesize a wrong item, or if one of your favorite bracelets crack.
Erasing seals costs 1000 Gitan, and mending bracelets costs 500 Gitan.

Also remember, you can select Shuten Trail Easy or Shuten Trail Mid
from Shuten Halfway if you're finding Shuten Trail Hard to be too difficult.

09F Cliff Wind Hole

Mixer makes an appearance, and with it comes the ability to synthesize equipment.
Note that Mixer's HP and attack increases with each item it swallows.
Use items like Dragon Grass, Decoy / Slumber Scrolls, or Knockback Staff to avoid damage.

Synthesizing a Herb, Otogiriso, and Life Grass onto a weapon will transform it into a Healing Sword.
It has an exceptional ability that restores HP equal to 1/3 of the damage you deal to monsters.
Try making it if you have the items on hand, or come back for it later.

"Perfect" grade materials can be found for the remainder of the dungeon.
They take up 2 inventory slots, so you may struggle to carry many if you don't have Mamo with you.
Note that Mamo can carry up to 5 "Perfect" grade materials, if you utilize all of their inventory space.
