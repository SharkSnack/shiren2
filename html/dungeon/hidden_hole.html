Shrine's Hidden Hole

Dungeon you can enter after clearing Onigashima.

Text

※ ※ ※ Warning ※ ※ ※

This dungeon loops endlessly after going down the stairs on 99F,
but Escape Scrolls are only available during the initial descent.
So if you want to go home at some point, you'll need to pick up the
Escape Scroll on 10F, 20F, or 99F before the dungeon loops.

If a Kappa throws the scroll at you, or you fall into a pitfall on 99F
before you obtain the scroll, you'll have to intentionally collapse or
select Suspend → Return to Village menu options to exit the dungeon.
