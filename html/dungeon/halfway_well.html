Halfway Well

Dungeon you can enter after registering 30 or more monsters to Monster Kingdom.

Shiren won't trigger traps by stepping on them in this dungeon,
but he can instead pick them up and set them on the ground to trap monsters.
Once a trap is set, it cannot be picked up again.
Traps have a chance to break after being triggered by monsters.
Lastly, note that Shiren can set traps down without ending his turn.
