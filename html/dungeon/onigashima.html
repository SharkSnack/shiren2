Onigashima

Dungeon you can enter after completing the castle.
Shiren and his allies go separate ways to avoid being
attacked as a group, with the goal of meeting up inside.

You could say this is where the game truly begins, with
the appearance of Monster Houses, more unidentified
items including scrolls, and a far deeper dungeon without
the safety of an interval area like Shuten Halfway.

Adding to the difficulty is a lower carry-in limit of 7 items.
Think carefully about what items you want to have
on hand, and use them to the best of your ability.

There is a boss fight against Galaha on 16F, and
then a final showdown against Boss Oni on 25F.
Defeating Boss Oni wraps up the main story and
shows the ending, and your save data gains an icon.

Dungeon Overview

Four key points:

  1. All Floors are random dungeon style maps with fog.

  2. Monster houses from 3F.

  3. New traps like flood, monster, pitfall.

  4. No allies first time, but can join up.
    Mamo retrieves lost equipment before post-game
    with a 25% upgrade value / seal penalty.
