Sealed Room

Dungeon you can enter after clearing Onigashima and visiting Shuten Summit.
Strong monsters appear from 1F, but you can bring allies and up to 11 items into the dungeon.
Be careful not to let your guard down even with an assortment of powerful items on hand.

The main objective is to rescue Shuten children who got lost inside the dungeon.
You'll be rewarded with an Ad Balloon castle decoration and a
Shopkeeper icon for your save file after rescuing all 5 children.

The children can be found on 20F, 30F, 40F, 50F, and 60F.
However, the dungeon actually continues infinitely even after 99F, as it begins a new loop.
An Escape Scroll is guaranteed to be on the ground on 99F, including 99F in subsequent loops.

You'll need to make multiple trips to the 70~80F Golden Interval if you want to complete the
equipment gallery, since both Dodge Shield and Reflect Shiled are treasure chest exclusives.

Note that you won't be able to enter Sealed Room until your
next visit to the Summit if you process castle parts.

Dungeon Overview

Shiren's stats will be reset when he enters the Sealed Room. (Level 1, strength 8/8, etc.)

This dungeon generally has less items per floor than others, and often has Gitan or negative
effect items like Unlucky Seed that can be thrown by Water Striders or Kappa family monsters.
Throw grasses at enemies or insert them into Identify Pots to identify them, rather than eating them.

Shops and Traders don't appear in this dungeon, but on rare occasions
you'll come across a Special Monster House called a Hawker House.
Hawkers are level 2 versions of Traders, and sometime have Angel Seeds for sale,
so it's recommended to bring some Gitan with you in case you run into one.

Note that Special Monster Houses no long appear after 88F.
