How do I play Shiren 2 in English?

The English Patch hasn't been released yet.
Keep on eye on the project thread for the latest news regarding the patch.

Anything I should know before playing?

  • There's a glitch that hard-locks the game, so it would be a good idea to read through the Glitches page.
  • Asuka and Mamo will lose their items if [Return to Napus Village] is selected at the main menu.
    This applies even if Asuka and Mamo weren't brought along for the current adventure.
    So if you don't want them to lose their items, don't select [Return to Napus Village].
    However, if the game freezes, you won't have any other option than to select [Return to Napus Village].
    So it's best to keep important items in storage, rather than having Asuka or Mamo carry them.
    Note that copying a journal will also delete Asuka and Mamo's items in the copy.

Does the 回 seal stack?

No, the 回 seal (Healing Sword) does not stack. So only use one seal.

Does the 弟 seal stack?

Yes, having multiple 弟 seals (Otogiriso) will increase the amount of HP that is restored.
Note that it restores HP separately from the 回 seal, so they can be used together.

Do seals need to be next to each other to stack?

No, the order of seals doesn't matter for stacked effects.

Where can I find Perfect Wood?

Perfect Wood can only be found in Shuten Trail Mid (8F~).
It cannot be obtained in Shuten Trail Hard.

What monster should I capture in Road to the End?

It's best to capture an Armordillo family monster, so that you can collect items at will.
Using this strategy takes a long time, but it's one of the most consistent ways to clear Road to the End.

However, Armordillo monsters are rather weak and prone to accidents,
so it's a good idea to capture a second one if you happen to come across a second Monster Pot.
Armordillo Pop is ideal, but an Armordillo Bro can also be used if you're careful.

Other noteworthy monsters include Monsoon Doll, Gazelle Pope, Hyper Gazer, and Shihan.

Do you lose upgrade values and seals when making Fiery Fuuma?

Yes, upgrade value and seals will be lost when the Katana+99 is transformed into a Fiery Fuuma.

This is also the case when the following weapons or shields are created:
Kaburasutegi, Daisangen Sword, Ryujin Sword, Wonder Pickaxe, Healing Sword,
Helix Shield, Daisangen Shield, Nirvana Shield.
