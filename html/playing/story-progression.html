Story

Shiren 2 is the story of Shiren when he was still only a child.
It's the first game in the timeline, and introduces some of the recurring characters in the series.

The story begins with wanderer Shiren and talking ferret Koppa traveling together.
They come across Napus Village, where the villagers are seemingly troubled by a clan of Oni.
Due to a strange coincidence, Shiren ends up having to build a castle to protect the village.
He heads off to Shuten Mountain which is full of monsters in search of castle materials.

Shiren finally completes the castle with help from the villagers.
With the castle standing tall, he heads to Onigashima with his allies to teach the Oni a lesson.
Onigashima is full of monsters, but he makes it to the top, and duels Boss Oni.
Shiren won the fight after a fierce battle, and peace finally arrived to Napus Village.

Upon returning to Napus Village, he is told of a prophecy.
"He will lead a smelly ferret and head toward numerous new dungeons..."
New dungeons appear, and Shiren's adventure is just getting started.

Characters

Shiren

The protagonist of Shiren 2.
He wears an iconic straw hat and a cape with vertical stripes,
and travels the countryside with his partner Koppa.
Shiren is 10 years old in this entry, and Shiren 2 is the first game in the timeline.

Koppa

A talking ferret who travels the countryside together with his partner Shiren.
He tends to speak in place of Shiren, and can be quite sarcastic at times.
Inside dungeons, he hangs out either in Shiren's hat or inside his bag.

Shiren is 18 years old in Shiren 1,
and it's mentioned that he and Koppa teamed up half a year ago in that game.
This seems inconsistent, since they've been together since at least when Shiren was 10 years old.
However, an official tweet exists that says there's an explanation that makes sense.

Himakichi

A Bored Kappa family monster who has more time than he knows what to do with.
He becomes interested in Shiren's fight against the Oni, and joins him to waste some time.
He has the ability to toss items at enemies, including arrows and Cannonballs.

Note that Himakichi isn't the same character as the Bored Kappa family ally seen in Asuka Kenzan.

Riku

Younger brother of the village beauty, Sasumi.
He was a delicate child, but grew stronger as a result of meeting Shiren.
He can shoot a slingshot in a 2 tile radius during battle.
However, he has the weakest stats as an ally, and tends to be more of a liability than a help.

Asuka

A 14 year old swordswoman who is undergoing training as a wanderer.
She tends to sound a little old-fashioned in the way she speaks.
She was helped by Shiren when she was disarmed and surrounded by Kengo at Shuten Halfway,
and decides to help him build the castle as thanks.

She's a popular character of the series, and became the protagonist of a sequel called Asuka Kenzan.
She also makes an appearance in Shiren 3 as an ally.

Kirara

Daughter of Boss Oni, who was rescued by Shiren when she was caught in a trap.
She had a difficult time being straight with her words,
but finally opens her heart after attending the village festival.
She develops a crush on Shiren, and later joins him as an ally.
She has the ability to beathe fire, and is immune to fire damage.

Main Story

Small Valley

Shiren rescues Kirara from a trap after clearing Small Valley.

Shuten Trail Easy

Event occurs at the udon restaurant in Napus Village, leading to Shiren having to build a castle.
The game automatically proceeds to Shuten Trail Easy after the events.

Shuten Trail Mid

Unlocked during an event that plays after clearing Shuten Trail Easy.
Shiren and Koppa obtain the Torn Manuscript if they defeat the 3 Water Striders on 13F.

Shuten Trail Hard

Talk to Saigo at the beach after clearing Shuten Trail Mid to turn the Torn Manuscript into the Ancient Manuscript.
Place the Ancient Manuscript in the small shrine in Napus Village (upper-right) to unlock Shuten Trail Hard.

Until Castle Completion

It's possible to progress the castle events without ever going to Shuten Trail Mid or Shuten Trail Hard.

Allies Joining Requirements

Himakichi
Joins after 6 castle parts + 4 or more adventures.
Randomly appears in the river in Napus Village after the inital encounter.
Asuka
Joins after 8 castle parts + 6 or more adventures.
Randomly appears at Shuten Halfway after the inital encounter..
Riku
Joins after 10 castle parts.
Randomly asks to join when leaving Napus Village after the inital encounter.
Kirara
Joins after 16 castle parts.
Randomly appears on 1~2F inside a dungeon after the inital encounter.
Mamo
Joins after 14 castle parts + 7 or more adventures.
Randomly appears outside the udon restaurant after the inital encounter.

The number of adventures requirement seems to fluctuate based on the number of completed castle parts.
It's assumed that the requirements are [X number of castle parts + Y number of adventures].
Usually all allies will have joined before the story progresses to attacking Onigashima.

All allies will be permanently available in Napus Village after clearing Onigashima.

Onigashima

Shiren, his allies, and the villagers launch an attack on Onigashima to teach the Oni a lesson.
Allies can appear inside Onigashima, and will ask to join you if you talk to them.

The ending will play and the credits will roll after defeating Boss Oni.
However, new post-game stories and dungeons open up when you resume your adventure.
So the main story might be over after Onigashima, but the game is just getting started.

Post-Game

Shrine's Hidden Hole and Sealed Room dungeons are now available.

Halfway Well is unlocked once 30 monsters have been registered to Monster Kingdom.

Road to the End is unlocked after clearing Halfway Well.
