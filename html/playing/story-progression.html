Main Story

Small Valley

Shiren rescues Kirara from a trap after clearing Small Valley.

Shuten Trail Easy

Event occurs at the udon restaurant in Napus Village, leading to Shiren having to build a castle.
The game automatically proceeds to Shuten Trail Easy after the events.

Shuten Trail Mid

Unlocked during an event that plays after clearing Shuten Trail Easy.
Shiren and Koppa obtain the Torn Manuscript if they defeat the 3 Water Striders on 13F.

Shuten Trail Hard

Talk to Saigo at the beach after clearing Shuten Trail Mid to turn the Torn Manuscript into the Ancient Manuscript.
Place the Ancient Manuscript in the small shrine in Napus Village (upper-right) to unlock Shuten Trail Hard.

Until Castle Completion

It's possible to progress the castle events without ever going to Shuten Trail Mid or Shuten Trail Hard.

Allies Joining Requirements

Himakichi
Joins after 6 castle parts + 4 or more adventures.
Randomly appears in the river in Napus Village after the inital encounter.
Asuka
Joins after 8 castle parts + 6 or more adventures.
Randomly appears at Shuten Halfway after the inital encounter..
Riku
Joins after 10 castle parts.
Randomly asks to join when leaving Napus Village after the inital encounter.
Kirara
Joins after 16 castle parts.
Randomly appears on 1~2F inside a dungeon after the inital encounter.
Mamo
Joins after 14 castle parts + 7 or more adventures.
Randomly appears outside the udon restaurant after the inital encounter.

The number of adventures requirement seems to fluctuate based on the number of completed castle parts.
It's assumed that the requirements are [X number of castle parts + Y number of adventures].
Usually all allies will have joined before the story progresses to attacking Onigashima.

All allies will be permanently available in Napus Village after clearing Onigashima.

Onigashima

Shiren, his allies, and the villagers launch an attack on Onigashima to teach the Oni a lesson.
Allies can appear inside Onigashima, and will ask to join you if you talk to them.

The ending will play and the credits will roll after defeating Boss Oni.
However, new post-game stories and dungeons open up when you resume your adventure.
So the main story might be over after Onigashima, but the game is just getting started.

Post-Game

Shrine's Hidden Hole and Sealed Room dungeons are now available.

Halfway Well is unlocked once 30 monsters have been registered to Monster Kingdom.

Road to the End is unlocked after clearing Halfway Well.
