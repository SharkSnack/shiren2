Secret Phrases

Secret phrases are passwords that can be told to Sir Victory Daruma inside the shrine for various rewards.
They can be used to obtain powerful equipment such as Homing Blade as early as your second adventure,
but doing so will break the balance of the main story dungeons, so it's not recommended.

All items obtainable through secret phrases can be obtained through regular gameplay,
though some items cannot be found until post-game dungeons.

Secret phrases have the following restrictions:

  • Each secret phrase can only be used once per save file.
  • Only one secret phrase can be used per adventure.
    So you won't be able to use another one until you either collapse, or clear a dungeon.

※ More English secret phrases will be revealed a little while after the patch is released.
