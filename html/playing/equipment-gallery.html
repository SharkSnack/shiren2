Equipment Gallery

The equipment gallery is a feature where you can decorate a wall with equipment you've obtained.
It can be accessed by talking to the Curator (leftmost NPC) inside Napus Storage.

An important note is that once equipment is displayed, it can never be taken down from the wall.
Ideally you'll want to display duplicate or cursed weapons and shields, rather than strengthened ones.

Nothing special happens upon completion, so this feature is just for collection and personal enjoyment.
